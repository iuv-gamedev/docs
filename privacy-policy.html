Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 27/02/2025

Thank you for playing our game(s)! This Privacy Policy applies to all games developed and published by IUV GameDev (“we,” “our,” or “us”) and outlines how we handle your privacy when you play our games.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information directly from users. However, we use third-party services such as Google AdMob to show advertisements. These services may collect limited device information such as your Android Advertising ID for ad delivery and analytics.

2. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads within our apps. These ads may be personalized based on your device data. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to collect and process this data to provide relevant ads.

Data collected through AdMob is handled in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. User Consent & Opting Out

Since we do not collect personal data ourselves, no opt-out is necessary. For more information about how Google handles your data and how to control ad personalization, you may visit the following links:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Google Ads Technology
  • Google Privacy & Partners
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Permissions

Our games may request the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to load advertisements.
  • Post Notifications: Used to send occasional reminders about the game. No personal data is collected or stored through this permission.

We do not request or use any other device permissions beyond what is required for gameplay and basic functionality.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our games are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will take steps to delete it. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at iuv.gamedev@gmail.com.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the integrity and security of our apps and the third-party services we use. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Please review this page regularly for updates.
